When You Should Wash Your Hands Helpful

You should wash your hands especially during these key times...

• Before, during, and after preparing food

• Before eating food

• Before and after treated a cut or wound

• After using the washroom

• After blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing

• After touchng an animal

• After handing pet food or pet treats

• After touching garbage

Reduce Sickness?

Remember that cleaning your hands can reduce your risk of getting sick, as well as the people around you!
